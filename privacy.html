Privacy Policy

security Trust & Privacy

This initiative is built on trust and community. We believe that accessing educational resources should not come at the cost of your personal data.

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies, cross-site trackers, or advertising pixels. You will not find any annoying cookie consent banners here because there is nothing to consent to.

Analytics

To understand which study materials are most helpful to our classmates, we use Cloudflare Web Analytics. This is a privacy-first tool that only counts basic page views and the country of origin. It is completely anonymized and cannot be tied back to your identity, IP address, or device.

Third-Party Links

The MLSU study notes and ZIP files are hosted via secure Google Drive links. When you click download, you are accessing the file directly through Google's infrastructure, subject to their standard security protocols.
